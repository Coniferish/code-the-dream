Explore the weather in Arctic Dreams by Barry Lopez

These charts are intended to provide more context for those reading Arctic Dreams. Published in 1986, Arctic Dreams describes four years of Barry Lopez's travels in the Alaskan and Canadian Arctic where he worked as a biologist. These charts show the current temperatures for two regions, the Davis and Bering Strait alongside historical data during the years in which Barry Lopez was presumably in these areas writing his book.
